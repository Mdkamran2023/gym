THE COMPANY & ITS ORIGIN:

MBS GYMKHANA LTD. is a Company incorporated under The Companies Act 1956 on 23.01.2008 with the main objective to establish natural house centers for building positive health (physical, moral, social and spiritual), through setting up a chain of high end Gymnasium and Spa Centres for self–development, under the brand name FITPUNKS at various other locations in Jaipur, Delhi and NCR.

MARKET POTENTIAL:

The core population of India lies in the age group of 20 to 45. With the increase in the number of educated young professionals who are not only capable of maintaining a good standard of living but are also health conscious, and are catching up with the habit of joining health and fitness centers. There is a huge scope for this business in the Delhi, NCR region for at least next two decades. Through its fifteen operating centers and five others in pipeline, the Company already has a strong customer base of near 10,000 members within Delhi and around. Thus, the Company has carved a niche for itself into retail services segment.
